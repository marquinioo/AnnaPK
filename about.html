About

The Art of Flowers

Meet Anna

With over a decade of experience in floral artistry, Anna brings a unique perspective to every arrangement she creates. Trained in classical European techniques and inspired by the organic beauty of nature, her work bridges the gap between tradition and contemporary design.

"I believe that flowers have an extraordinary ability to transform spaces and touch hearts. Each stem tells a story, and my role is to weave these stories into something meaningful and memorable."

Based in the heart of the city, Anna PK Floral Design has become synonymous with sophisticated, thoughtfully crafted floral experiences. From intimate dinner parties to lavish weddings, we approach each project with the same dedication to excellence.

Our Philosophy

Rooted in Nature, Refined by Art

Seasonal Beauty

We work with what nature provides, embracing the unique beauty of each season. This approach ensures the freshest blooms and the most authentic arrangements.

Sustainable Practice

From locally sourced flowers to eco-conscious materials, sustainability is woven into every aspect of our work. Beauty should never come at the expense of our planet.

Personal Touch

Every client receives our undivided attention. We take the time to understand your vision, preferences, and the emotions you wish to evoke.
